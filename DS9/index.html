Welcome to Deep Space 9!

Visit Deep Space Nine for a memorable pitstop along your interstellar travel! Experience everything from dabo to unique astronomical phenomena today!

Deep Space 9 or DS9 for short, originally known as Terok Nor, is located in the Denorious belt in the Bajoran system of space. It is currently an active space station owned by the Bajoran Republic and operated by both Bajoran Militia and Starfleet. The station was one of the most historically, politically, and strategically important space stations in the Alpha Quadrant during the latter half of the 24th century.

Attractions

The Wormhole

The most popular attraction, followed shortly by Quark’s is the Wormhole! Listed by trip advisor as number 1 reason to visit Deep Space Nine, visitors from all over the galaxy flock to glimpse at this rare phenomena!

The Bajoran wormhole was the only stable wormhole known to exist in the Milky Way Galaxy. It was the home of extra-dimensional beings who were worshiped by the Bajorans as gods, known as the Prophets. Before the wormhole was discovered by Commander Benjamin Sisko and Lieutenant Jadzia Dax in 2369, it was considered to be a myth.

Take some time to see Bajor!

Quark's Bar

By far the most popular attraction to Deep Space Nine is Quark’s Bar, located right on our promenade. Species from both the Alpha and Gamma Quadrant flock to Quark’s for a spin at the Dabo Wheel or to experience the latest holodeck simulation!
